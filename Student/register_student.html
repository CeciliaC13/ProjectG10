Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms

By registering an account and using the UTMSPACE College Booking System, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use our services.

2. Eligibility

To use our services, you must:

  • Be a registered student of Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM)
  • Have a valid UTM email address (@utm.my or @graduate.utm.my)
  • Be at least 18 years old or have parental consent
  • Provide accurate and complete registration information

3. Booking and Payment

When you make a room booking through our system:

  • All bookings are subject to availability and college quotas
  • Payment must be completed within 3 days of key collection
  • Failure to pay within the deadline may result in automatic cancellation
  • All payments are processed through ToyyibPay and are non-refundable after confirmation

4. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your booking at any time before payment. Cancelled bookings will make the room available for other students. No refunds will be issued for cancellations after payment has been processed.

5. User Responsibilities

As a user of this system, you agree to:

  • Keep your account credentials secure and confidential
  • Not share your account with others
  • Provide truthful and accurate information
  • Not misuse or attempt to disrupt the system
  • Follow all college and hostel regulations

6. Prohibited Items and Conduct

Students are strictly prohibited from bringing prohibited appliances as listed in the Appliance Declaration Form. Violation may result in disciplinary action or fines.

7. Account Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate your account if you violate these terms, provide false information, or misuse the system. You may also delete your account at any time by contacting support.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you register an account with UTMSPACE College Booking System, we collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Student ID / Matric Number
  • Email Address (UTM email)
  • Phone Number
  • Gender
  • Booking and payment history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your room booking requests
  • Communicate with you about your bookings and status updates
  • Verify your identity as a UTM student
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important notifications regarding your reservations

3. Data Protection

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your password is encrypted and stored securely.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared only with:

  • College administration for verification purposes
  • Payment processing partners (ToyyibPay) to complete transactions
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your login session, and analyze site traffic. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of certain features.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account (subject to pending obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
